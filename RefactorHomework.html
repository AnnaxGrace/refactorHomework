California Restaurants can now sell alcohol to go. Alcoholic Beverage Control (ABC) now lets restaurants to sell beer, wine, and pre-mixed drinks for pickup or delivery in California.


About Us

The Potholder Cafe is the undisputed "King" of Long Beach when it comes to breakfast, which we proudly serve all day. Weekday mornings and early afternoons find plenty of starving artists and blue-collar diners wolfing down stacks of pancakes and bacon, and you’ll see the occasional yuppie munching down a Chinese Chicken Salad from the healthy side of the menu. Weekends are packed with hungry friends and families enjoying the atmosphere and history of this Belmont Heights landmark.
